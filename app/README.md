### Features 
#### Currency Conversion including USD, AUD, EUR, JPY, GBP.

Conversion Rates:

- 1 USD = 1.55 AUD
- 1 USD = 0.92 EUR
- 1 USD = 148.50 JPY
- 1 USD = 0.78 GBP


#### Fuel Efficiency and Distance Conversion (mpg, km/L, Litres, Nautical Miles)
Fuel & Distance: 

- 1 mpg = 0.425 km/L
- 1 Gallon (US) = 3.785 Litres
- 1 Nautical Mile = 1.852 Kilometres

#### Temperature Conversion including Clesius, Farhrenheit and Kelvin.

Temperature:

- Celsius to Fahrenheit: F = (C × 1.8) + 32
- Fahrenheit to Celsius: C = (F − 32) / 1.8
- Celsius to Kelvin: K = C + 273.15
